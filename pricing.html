What's included:

(2) Lead shooters

Shot in 8K/4K, delivered in 4K

All-day (3) camera shoot of your day's important events

Drone coverage

Yes we provide drone coverage! We fly them ourselves, only when it's safe for everyone, and do not outsource. Usually, we can find a window in the day, even if it's raining, to get it up in the sky. Things that will prevent drone coverage: high winds, rain, mist and available space to fly in.

Delivery within (30) days

Our contract reads (90) days but normally our turnaround is much faster - usually, within a week or two, depending on a variety of factors. Since we shoot at very high resolutions, it can take a week for films to render (upload to sites, ect.).

Things we don't do:

Provide "Raw Footage"

Since we shoot at very high resolutions in a "flat" profile there is no practical way to supply couples with the "raw footage". We also do not shoot our wedding films in a consumer format so the couple would need a commercial-level computer and software suite to view footage. However, we do communicate with all couples before hand, multiple times, to make sure if there is any event that is out-of-the-ordinary they want captured in full, we get it for them! The above image shows what raw footage looks like vs the finished product; the bottom half is how the footage looks, directly off the camera.

Use on- and off-camera lights

We choose not to light any couple with anything except available light. We've found this makes the couple feel more comfortable, along with their guests. Static lighting also tends to kill the dance floor vibe!

Fight with your photographer or vendors

Your day is about the couple, not the vendors. We make sure to touch base with all vendors when possible before hand, especially the photographer. This way, we can coordinate and get a vibe for how they want to work.

Pose you for hours

We work with the photographer to make sure they get everything they need first, shooting around them. Usually, in the time they are taking to move flashes or change a lens, ect. we will shoot our footage. We love to get the couple out around sunset for a quick session when possible and will coordinate this with your photographer.

Use unflattering footage

We only include the best footage in your full length film and trailer. What this means is, normally, there isn't a lot of "getting ready" footage in our full-films, as it is reserved in small clips for the trailer. Again, every wedding is different and we will communicate directly with the couple before-hand to make sure we get all the coverage.

Charge you for travel

Except in extreme circumstances, we'll never charge you for our travel time!

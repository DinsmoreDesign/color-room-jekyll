Things we don't do:

Provide "Raw Footage"

Since we shoot at very high resolutions in a "flat" profile there is no practical way to supply couples with the "raw footage". We also do not shoot our wedding films in a consumer format so the couple would need a commercial-level computer and software suite to view footage. However, we do communicate with all couples before hand, multiple times, to make sure if there is any event that is out-of-the-ordinary they want captured in full, we get it for them! Use on- and off-camera lights

We choose not to light any couple with anything except available light. We've found this makes the couple feel more comfortable, along with their guests. Static lighting also tends to kill the dance floor vibe!

Fight with your photographer or vendors

Your day is about you, the couple, not the vendors. We make sure to touch base with all vendors when possible before hand, especially the photographer. This way, we can coordinate and get a vibe for how they want to work.

Pose you for hours

We work with the photographer to make sure they get everything they need first, shooting around them. Usually, in the time they are taking to move flashes or change a lens, ect. we will shoot our footage. We love to get the couple out around sunset for a quick session when possible and will coordinate this with your photographer.

Use unflattering footage

We only include the best footage in your full length film and trailer. What this means is, normally, there isn't a lot of "getting ready" footage in our full-films, as it is reserved in small clips for the trailer. Again, every wedding is different and we will communicate directly with the couple before-hand to make sure we get all the coverage.

Charge you for travel

Except in extreme circumstances, we'll never charge you for our travel time!

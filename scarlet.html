What's included:

(1) Lead + (1) Documentary Shooter

You will receive 2 shooters all day, one focused on main content shots and the other on capturing all the special behind-the-scenes moments.

10 hours of coverage

Normally we try to start about a half hour before photo starts. This allows the photographer to have time with "the details" alone if they would like. We will communicate before hand with the couple and the photographer to see what the perfect schedule would be. Every wedding is different so we don't have a hard and fast contracted time in our quotes

Shot in 5K/4K, delivered in 4K

By shooting in higher resolutions we are able to not use intrusive lighting, while producing the most flattering motion pictures possible.

All-day (3) camera shoot of your day's important events

We use (2) static 4K cameras on tripods will be moved around depending on your venue and (1) 5K RED Scarlet-W that we use to shoot your trailer footage. Everything we use is compact, so we can stay out of your way. We never use lights, to make sure everyone is comfortable in front of the camera. We do our best to never bring any attention to us and to stay out of the photographer's way by shooting with them. We never want to get in the way of guests or your photographers.

Multiple delivery formats

We use MediaZilla as it provides the easiest way to download, burn DVD/Blu Ray and view from your computer. Also included is a Vimeo Portfolio in 4K of your films, which is best for viewing on a phone or streaming via your smart TV, Apple TV or Roku. Here is an example of MediaZilla's package as well as a Vimeo Portfolio .

(1) Trailer and full-length film

Normally, we use 1 or 2 songs for your trailer (we purchase the publishing for the music that's used exclusively for your wedding), however, since we don't do cookie cutter trailers, this number may change in edit. Full films generally range from 1 to 2 hours depending on a variety of factors including ceremony length, first look, intros, ect. Here is a trailer example as well as a full-length example .

Delivery within (30) days

Our contract reads (90) days but normally our turnaround is much faster - usually, within a week or two, depending on a variety of factors. Since we shoot at very high resolutions, it can take a week for films to render (upload to sites, ect.).

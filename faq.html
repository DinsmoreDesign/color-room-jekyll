Here's a few answers to our most frequently asked questions.

What makes us different?

Since every wedding is unique so is every video. We don't just slap a preset on your footage and call it a day. We bring your footage to life by color correcting and grading your footage to give the your wedding that classic Hollywood look.

How much coverage is included?

10 - 12 hours. Normally we try to start about a half hour before photo starts. We will communicate before-hand with the couple and the photographer to make sure the schedule works perfectly. Every wedding is different so we don't have a hard and fast contracted time in our quotes.

Do you shoot any video with drones?

Yes we provide drone coverage! We fly them ourselves, only when it's safe for everyone, and do not outsource. Usually, we can find a window in the day, even if it's raining, to get our drone up in the sky. Things that will prevent drone coverage: high winds, rain, mist and available space to fly in.

When do I get my film?

Normally we can have a full finished wedding trailer to you in about a month, sometimes less. If you have a deadline you want to meet please let us know ahead of time, we have never made a bride wait past a deadline for her film.

Do you charge for travel?

Except in extreme circumstances, we'll never charge you for our travel time!

How do I get my wedding film?

We deliver our wedding films using Mediazilla.com. You can view, download and even burn a DVD from your custom link. USB and Custom DVD/Blu Ray options are available as add-ons. Please contact us for pricing.
